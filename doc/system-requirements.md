# System Requirements

## Actors

- Online Customer
  - Manages online reservations
  - Reads location data, such as menu, Waiter reviews, operating hours, contact info, etc.
- Onsite Customer
  - He takes a table, orders, consumes what the restaurant has to offer (drinks, food, etc.)
  - Makes payments, requests, and Waiter calls
  - Personalizes his experience by changing his Waiter, table, or buffer management
- Waiter
  - Staff needed to serve the Customers
  - Each one has a personalized profile integrating reviews
- Owner
  - Owns the business/location
  - Decides Waiter management
  - Responsible for menu correctness and updates
  - In some cases, one or more of their responsibilities can be given to a Waiter

## Software Requirements

- Backend service and code
- Physical QR codes printed for each table
- Frontend/interface for all actors
- Database: accounts, menu

## Customer Requirements

- Device having a minimal camera with QR scanning software
- Internet access

## Waiter Requirements

- Device having a minimal display where they can see and navigate table details
- Internet access
- Email address

## Owner requirements

- Device having a minimal display where they can see and navigate location details
- Internet access
- Email address
- Physical location
- Internet router (Optionally an Internet repeater)
- Waiters
- Tables
- Menu
- Infrastructure, real servers and nodes for this system
