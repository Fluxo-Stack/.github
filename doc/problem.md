# Problem

## Overview for Location Owners

The objective is to create a consistent and predictable experience for bar and restaurant guests, recognizing that they may arrive in very **different states of mind**. At times, they may feel stressed or rushed, wondering, “Has anyone noticed us yet?” Other times, they may prefer more time before ordering, thinking, “I hope he doesn’t come back again to ask if we’re ready.” And occasionally, they may not want any human interaction at all.

Our goal is to give guests a new kind of control: the ability to choose how and when they engage. We believe this empowerment can significantly enhance **customer satisfaction**. By guiding and structuring communication between guests and staff, we can maximize comfort and create a more personalized dining experience.

Critically, this is a **human-centered** hybrid solution. We are not replacing personal hospitality; we are augmenting it. The platform is designed to **coexist with traditional service models**, ensuring that guests who prefer physical menus and cash payments can continue their experience without friction. We believe that by putting the user in control of the "interface" of their meal, we deliver a more inclusive and sophisticated service environment.

The core of our system involves multiple key actors, all considered our clients. Our development philosophy is 100% client-centric, focusing on their experience and driving our work backward from their specific requirements and feedback. The development roadmap prioritizes functionalities for **Customers**, followed by those for **Waiters**, and finally for **Owners**.

Keywords: Customer, Waiter, Owner, Reservation, Buffet, Table, Menu, Product (Dish, Drink), Delay

## Key benefits

- **Customer comfort**
  - Time management
  - Social management - personalized interaction with the Waiters
- **Real time requests/responses** for Customers and Waiters
- Full control over the business
  - Easy to update table count integrated our system for **smooth adaptation**
  - **Flexible Waiter management **allowing both the system mechanism or manual configuration
- Real time, efficient menu updates - each new specialty won’t require reprinting the physical menus - cutting printing prices (See Acaju - “Amestecul de miercuri”)

# Features

The MVP is constituted by all the non-optional features. Each feature is described further in the User Stories/Solution tabs.

## Customer Features

- Tour/tutorials for **smooth adaptation** (**optional**)
- Make/Cancel a reservation
- **Call a Waiter**
- Choose or request to change the Waiter (**optional**)
- Choose or request to change/merge tables (**optional**)
- View menu
- Select and order products
- Add notes for a specific product/dish (**optional**)
- Add reviews for the consumed dishes (**optional**)
- Checkout (selects the paying method)
- Payments/tips within the app (**optional**)
  - Select any number of cards (feasible limitations apply)
- Review the Waiter (**optional**)
- Review the system (**optional**)

## Waiter Features

- Respond/call a delay for Customer requests
- Audio notification when assigned to a new table
- Time counter for each Customer since he placed the request
- Reminders to check upon Customers after a specific amount of time (**optional**)
- Profile management (updates, visibility (GDPR), closing)
- Close a table after checkout

## Owner Features

- Create/edit menu
- Create/edit offers and discounts (**optional**)
- Optimized workforce balance (LC algorithm) / manual Waiter management
- Personalized table integration in the system (optionally create a table map)
- Monthly analytics on the impact of the product over the business (**optional**)
- Closes the business (**optional**)
- Multiple location management (**optional**)
